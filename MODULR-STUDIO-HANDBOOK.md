## 1. What is Modulr Studio?
Modulr Studio is a high-performance, AI-driven architectural visualization platform built specifically for the outdoor living and garden room industry. Unlike generic AI tools, Modulr is trained on architectural geometry, lighting, and materials common to luxury timber and composite structures.

It bridges the gap between a 2D sketch and a professional 3D CGI, delivering photorealistic results in seconds instead of days.

## 2. Core Features & Studio Modes

### 📸 The Render Engine (Batch Mode)
The flagship feature of Modulr. It takes your site photos or CAD line drawings and breathes life into them.
- **Sequential 5-Angle Rendering**: Upload up to 5 images (Front, Sides, Back) to generate a cohesive portfolio.
- **Orientation Awareness**: The AI automatically detects if it's looking at a "Left Side" or "Front Elevation" and applies materials accordingly.
- **Filmstrip Gallery**: Seamlessly switch between all generated views for a 360-degree presentation.

### 📐 Creative Line Converter
Transform a simple hand-drawn sketch or even a text description into a clean, architectural line drawing (CAD Style).
- **Material-Aware Line Logic**: Can output flat-colored "blueprint" styles to help clients understand volume and scale.

### 🎨 Material Studio
A high-detail mode for material specialists.
- **Texture Analysis**: Automatically detects existing materials from a source photo.
- **2x2 Macro Cards**: Generates "Presentation Boards" showing close-up macro shots of your chosen cladding, roofing, and window finishes.

### ⛅ Weather Lab & Environment
Change the vibe of any project instantly.
- **Seasonal Shifts**: View a building in Summer Golden Hour, Winter Snow, or Autumn Rain.
- **Lighting Direction**: Precisely control the shadows to match the building's orientation on site.

## 3. The Technical Process (Under the Hood)

Modulr Studio uses a multi-layered AI stack to ensure accuracy and structural integrity:

1. **Spatial Analysis (Gemini 3.1 Pro)**: Every image is first analyzed by a vision model that determines the "bone structure" of the building and its orientation in 3D space.
2. **Nano Banana Pro Engine (V3.2)**: A custom-tuned rendering engine that applies PBR (Physically Based Rendering) materials. It ensures that "Black Charred Cladding" looks deep and rich, while "Zinc Roofing" maintains its metallic sheen. **Continuous Evolution**: These AI models are upgraded regularly (V3.0 to V3.1 to V3.2), meaning the accuracy and photorealism of your renders will naturally improve over time with **free automatic updates**.
3. **Sequential Queueing Logic**: To ensure stability on the cloud, Modulr processes batch renders in a "chain." This prevents server timeouts and ensures every single image gets the maximum processing power it deserves.
